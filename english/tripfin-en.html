This is an internship project in Trip.com Group in Finance BU. As the product manager, I transformed requirements from the business department into the actual landing project. The purpose of the project is to gradually converse the current 20000+ DAU into users who buy products through relevant operational activities and page revision.

After the launch of this page, the conversion rate from homepage to detail page increased about 22% in one week.

Background



Current Situation


From the perspective of the overall process, users are mainly lost during the process from the homepage to the details page, and the conversion rate is the lowest among all steps, only 32%
Although there are a large number of coupon recipients, the final coupon usage rate is only 5%, and about 30% of new users didn't use coupons to buy products

Requirements from the business department

1. Carry out new operational activity which is"double interest hike", in order to attract more newcomers through "double interest" strategy
2. Attract users to invest in products that need to be held for a long time
3. Only for new users who have not invested since the event was launched

Solutions


1.Optimize homepage layout
Distinguish current products and fixed deposit products
Add more useful information: Safety information, interest rate hike rules and buying process etc.


2.Cancel the issue of coupons and carry out regular operational activities instead
Omit coupons, and let the back-end system judge whether it meets the interest rate hike rules, and automatically issue reward
Apply the coupon rate hike to the operational activities, so that users can more flexibly choose the time to purchase the product, and it is also convenient for us to adjust the operation strategy at any time
Establish the seckill strategy to attract users to purchase products


3.Transition strategy for users who have received coupons before the event
Those who haven't used coupons before the event can choose to give up coupons and participate in the new event; they can also purchase the products with coupons at the original interest rate.

Summary





Eventually, with the joint efforts of UI designer's visual design and development, the event page was officially launched, and part of the interface is shown in the above two pictures. The conversion rate of the new account homepage-detail page in the first week after the launch has increased by 22%.
This is the first time that I have participated in the entire process of product requirements from proposal to launch. Different from teamwork projects in schools, when designing products in enterprises, in addition to covering all user usage scenarios and processes to complete the closed loop, it is also necessary to consider how to promote demand online as efficiently as possible. To achieve this, it is necessary to conduct in-depth communication with the engineers after product planning, understand the current back-end architecture and technically achievable content, and then reflect on which parts of the current design may require a large amount of development, which parts have higher priority and which parts can be completed in later iterations. After completing these thoughts, we can finally produce a product demand that can be successfully launched.
